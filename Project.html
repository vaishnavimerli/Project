MAJOR PROJECT

Gesture Controlled Robot

        Gesture recognization is a topic in science and technology with the goal of interpreting human gestures
        via mathematical algorithms.
        Gesture originate from  any bodily motion or state but most commonly through face and hands.
        Users can use simple gestures to communicate with the devices without physically touching them.
        Many approaches have been made using camera and computer vision algorithms to interrupt sign languages.
        However the identification and recognization of posture ,gait,proxemics and human is also the subject 
        of gestures recognization techniques. Gesture recognization is seen as a way for computer to begin 
        to understand human body language could make conventional input on devices such and even redundant.
